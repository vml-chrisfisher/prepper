Smoked Beets with Creme Fraiche and Herbs Salad
Cooking
There has never been a beet I didn’t like. This Smoked Beets with Creme Fraiche and Herbs salad is a play on the classic beets and goat cheese salad. Smoking the beets at a low temperature allows the sugars in the beets to caramelize and concentrate. Countered with the tang that comes from creme fraiche and balance just comes to play Beets can be a tough sell to some and if that’s the case, carrots work well with this recipe as well.
Using Cola On Your Compost Heap
Reading
We’ve all seen the video of Coke eating away at corrosion or even meat from a bone. So to find that its acid properties will actually help jumpstart the process of breaking down some of the plant material is a pleasant surprise and find. Using Cola On Your Compost Heap throws a bit of useful knowledge that will always play into the useless knowledge bucket as well.
Top 10 Bicycles Designed To Make Urban Commute Eco-Friendly
Reading
It’s hard to debate that biking is the most eco-friendly transportation we have to date. I’ve never been an avid biker, though having kids will get you on the kick again. Top 10 Bicycles Designed To Make Urban Commute Eco-Friendly shows bikes that are as beautiful as forwarded thinking.
Cotillions by William Patrick Corgan
Listening
In my book, Smashing Pumpkins and Stone Temple Pilots are two of the greatest bands in my history with Lenny Kravitz fulfilling the solo artist category. This last week, I found myself going back through a lot of that music and really falling down the rabbit hole with Billy Corgan’s solo work. Cotillions keep you close with Billy’s unique voice following melodies that you would have never found from the Pumpkin’s back in the day.
