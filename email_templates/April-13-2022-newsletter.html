Weekly Newsletter No. 31
Hello All,

Hope everyone is doing well. The weather this last weekend was amazing. It felt good to get back into the yard, clean up the beds and see the beginnings of Spring sprout the mulch. Lawns and gardening is a forever long game though drinking a beer after a good day of yard work bring one a sense of pride. Feeling you have tamed the black magic that is keeping a manicured lawn. It would be interesting to hear what everyone does to keep their lawns going. Let me know of any old tricks you have learned. And with that, let’s get to it.
Jammy Eggs
Cooking
Easter is this Sunday and eggs are always second on the menu for us. I’ve always been a fan of Deviled Eggs though Jammy Eggs are where I tend to land these days. They give you a blank slate to add all types of toppings.
10 Small Modern Cabins We’re Dreaming of Escaping To by Design Milk
Reading
Switching clients at work has made things move faster and calendar juggling with the wife a new sport. While this house tends to thrive in chaos it does tend to allow one to dream of a place of solitude. I have come to terms that I will always, but will probably never have, a place in the middle of the woods to allow for quiet. This is probably why 10 Small Modern Cabins We’re Dreaming of Escaping To by Design Milk grabbed my attention. No deep thinking just amazing small cabins in the middle of nowhere.
To Be Determined by The Main Squeeze
Listening
It’s really hard for me to place a comparison on The Main Squeeze. Though if you are looking for just a good soul-funk band that’s always moving these guys are it. There are moments of Pink Floyd and then their guitars can just make it wail to almost Prince levels. You will want these guys on your summer playlist.

That’ll do it folks. Be good to each other and enjoy the weekend.

Cheers,
Chris
