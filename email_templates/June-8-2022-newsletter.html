Weekly Newsletter No. 38
Happy Wednesday All,

Lately, it feels as though life is moving at a pretty good pace. One could say that Summer is in full roar but I tend to snuff this up to changing schedules and us getting used to riding this one out. Shifts always seem chaotic and just as you feel it smooth out another shift happens. Our little unit tends to lean into the chaos. I’m never sure if that is good or bad, but it keeps it all interesting. We are closing in on our trip to France and I’m still searching for that smoothness to pack and make sure I’m ready to go. As history tells it, it will come closer we get and the calm I was looking for tends to be more just a moment of less chaos compared to none. With that, let’s get to it.
Tigerlilly by Natalie Merchant
Listening
We spent the weekend celebrating a good friend’s birthday which took us all back to all the different forms of alternative music from the 90s. As we were driving home, one that seemed to keep us hitting repeat was Tigerlilly from Natalie Merchant. It played like an old friend and allowed for the conversation about the concert festival that started them all, Lollapalooza.
Saint Cloud One Year Anniversary Show by Waxahatchee
Listening
I didn’t mean to make this newsletter a music special, but here we are. This band doesn’t miss a beat. Waxahatchee always provides a lesson in rich simplicity and this video of the Saint Cloud One Year Anniversary Show gives you that aesthetic in sound and vision as well.

I realize this week leaves you with no recipes and I promise to bring that back next week. Be safe out there.

Cheers,
Chris
