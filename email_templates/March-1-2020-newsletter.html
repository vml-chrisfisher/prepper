Rebirth Brass Band
Listening
If there is one band that always takes me back to New Orleans it is Rebirth Brass Band. Founded by the Frazier Brothers, trumpeter Kermit Ruffins and their high school marching band they are known for being the first to add a lttle funk to their arrangements. It's a sound that you want when you think about NOLA.
