Home Cured Fresh Ham
Cooking
Easter always has me thinking about ham. If you ask me about a honey spiral cut ham, I may just walk away from you. I’ve been curing my own ham for years and I just won't go back to a store-bought cured ham, especially one with a glaze packet attached. This Cured Fresh Ham recipe takes some planning but is always comes out beautifully.
The Strategy of Conflict by Thomas C Schelling
Reading
One can’t ignore the way currently being waged between Ukraine and Russia. Our role as big brother in this conflict is an interesting one, fighting a proxy war with Russia. So it was interesting to find out that our strategy is based on The Strategy of Conflict, a series of essays by Thomas C. Schelling on the game theory where two adversaries have limited moves beyond both sides lose. Fascinating and interesting that it was written in the 1950s for this exact scenario.
