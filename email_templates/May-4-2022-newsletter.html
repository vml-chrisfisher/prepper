Hot Honey
Cooking
Hot Honey is absolutely a treat, and until you taste it, it’s hard to understand. It is often talked about as the power of sweet and savory. Add a bit of heat, and there is something that just hits.
Vanishing Half
Reading
My cousin sent me a text saying I needed to read Vanishing Half. I was immediately thrown into the mix of the dichotomy of being Creole and how that fits into society. It hops chronologically and is absolutely something my soul needed.
Watch My Moves by Kurt Vile
Listening
In the past, Kurt Viles was always an artist whose guitar movements hooked you like Tommy Petty. However, The Watch My Moves album feels next level. It’s as if the pandemic did him some good. There is something new in his voice, almost as if a new self-confidence has been released. I’m all in.
